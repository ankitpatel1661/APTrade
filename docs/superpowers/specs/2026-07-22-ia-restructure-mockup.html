New · Home The dashboard the spec always promised

Opens on "how am I doing" — value hero, day change, and a Today feed pulling from Calendar, Income, and your movers. The bell opens the new all-alerts list.

Regrouped · Markets Everything market-facing in one place

Watchlist is the default pill; Screener, Calendar, and News are one tap away. Try all four pills — each is mocked. A visible search field replaces the buried magnifier.

Slimmed · Portfolio Four sections, all about what you own

Holdings, Allocation, Activity, Performance — all four pills are mocked. Export moves here from the account menu. Same order on every platform.

Promoted · Invest Plans and Income become first-class

Both pills are mocked: pie cards with next contribution, and the income screen with the monthly chart (dashed projections) and the DRIP toggle moved beside its effect.

Desktop — macOS & Windows click every sidebar item

The same grouping as an always-visible left sidebar — all eleven destinations are mocked, plus Search (⌘K) opening the command palette. Opening a symbol keeps you in context (master–detail) instead of replacing the window.
